# MeteoSran v1.6.3 Release Summary

Welcome to MeteoSran v1.6.3! This release is a "Reliability & Resilience" milestone, focusing on making the chat experience bulletproof and fixing subtle race conditions in the conversation flow.

## 🛡️ "Bulletproof" Chat History
- **In-Memory Sorting**: We've bypassed Firestore's fragile indexing rules for chat messages. By fetching and sorting messages in-memory, we guarantee that your entire conversation timeline is always visible and perfectly chronological, regardless of how legacy data was stored.
- **Race Condition Fix**: Eliminated a "stale state" bug in the message sending flow. The app now uses a definitive, synchronous snapshot of your conversation history for both the UI and the AI, ensuring they never fall out of sync.

## 🖼️ Enhanced Image Intelligence
- **Silent Prompt Fallback**: When you upload a photo without typing a message, MeteoSran now automatically understands your intent. It uses a smart fallback prompt ("Could you analyze this image...") to ensure you always get a high-quality meteorological analysis, even when you're in a hurry.

## 🛠️ Technical Improvements
- **Regenerate Safety Guard**: Added a defensive check to the message regeneration system to prevent attempts at regenerating from non-user messages, avoiding silent failures.
- **Base64 Optimization**: Verified and polished the image encoding pipeline for 100% compatibility with Gemini's high-resolution vision processing.
- **Clean State Management**: Refactored internal React hooks in `App.tsx` for better performance and easier maintenance of the chat state.
